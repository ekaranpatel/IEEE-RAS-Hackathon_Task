About the Event

The IEEE RAS x IAS Hackathon is a premier 2-day event focused on solving real-world challenges in robotics and industrial applications. It brings together the brightest minds to build, design, and deploy innovative solutions.

Hackathon Tracks

  • Smart Automation Building systems that think for themselves and optimize industrial workflows.
  • Human-Robot Interaction Designing the intuitive and seamless interfaces of tomorrow.
  • Industrial IoT Connecting the smart factories of the future through robust networks.
